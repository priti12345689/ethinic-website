About Us

Ethinic Bhojpuri Music isn't just a label—it's a cultural heartbeat. We're preserving the legacy of Bhojpuri sound while amplifying modern voices to the world.

From folk classics to modern anthems, our platform empowers regional artists, connects communities, and celebrates our heritage through content and live experiences.
